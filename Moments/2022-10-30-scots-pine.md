sa ghiuthas-Albannach, eun a’ bruidhinn  
chan ann ann an cainnt nan eun  
ach anns a’ chànan aige fhèin 

in the Scots Pine, a bird talks not in the language of the birds but in his own idiolect

-- Rody Gorman
